# Diet Care Platform (DCP)

Diet Care Platform is an AI-powered digital health platform focused on
personalized clinical nutrition.

It connects patients, doctors, and dietitians through a shared ecosystem
that supports medical analysis, dietary planning, and long-term health management.

---

## Why Diet Care Platform?

Nutrition is deeply connected to health, yet dietary decisions are often made
without sufficient medical context, structure, or continuity.

Diet Care Platform addresses this gap by combining:
- medical data analysis
- AI-assisted nutrition planning
- collaboration with healthcare professionals
- continuous support for patients

---

## Who is this platform for?

- Patients seeking personalized, medically informed nutrition support
- Doctors and dietitians working with complex dietary and health data
- Health-tech professionals and researchers
- Institutions interested in preventive and nutritional healthcare

---

## Pediatric module

In 2026, development of a dedicated pediatric panel has begun.

The pediatric module is designed as a separate, purpose-built space,
created specifically for children and for supporting parents in making
informed, safe health and nutrition decisions.
